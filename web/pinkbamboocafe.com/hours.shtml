HOURS OPEN AT PINK BAMBOO HOT POT CAFE

Our hours of operation are as follows:
  • Sunday through Thursday from 11 am to 10 pm  (Yes, we ARE NOW OPEN on Tuesdays!)
  • Friday and Saturday from 11 am to 11 pm
Luncheon is served from 11 am to 3 pm.

Yes, we have take out service.  However, hot pot dining is only served at the restaurant.  Please call us about catering.  We can make deliveries within 3 miles of the restaurant.  Please ask us about delivery service when you call to place your order.

Please have at look at our menu selections.

We accept Visa, MasterCard and American Express credit cards.

If you have never tried hot pot dining, then plan on trying something new and exciting.  You may also enjoy your favorite beverage while at the hot pot tables.

We hope to see you soon at Pink Bamboo Hot Pot Cafe.
