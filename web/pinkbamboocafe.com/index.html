Pink Bamboo Cafe is now open online order! We OPEN 7 DAYS A WEEK for your dining pleasure.

Welcome to Pink Bamboo Hot Pot Cafe.  We are conveniently located at 128 Penhallow Street in Portsmouth, NH.  We offer hot pot dining as well as a wide variety of other menu items and our chef specialties.  We are now open Sunday through Thursday from 11 am to 10 pm, and Friday and Saturday from 11 am to 11 pm. We serve luncheon from 11 am to 3 pm each day.  We also offer take out service for most items.

The famous hot pot dining is served only in the restaurant.  Hot pot dining is served on our special tables which have a heating plate built into the table center.  A simmering bowl is placed on the heating plate and different ingredients are cooked in the hot pot by the person dining.  Some people called this form of dining "Chinese fondue" or "Steamboat."  The Chinese tradition of dining in this manner is over a thousand years old!  There are many options for hot pot dining, and we invite you to try them all from vegetarian, beef, pork, chicken, shrimp to other seafood items.  Of course, if hot pot dining is not for you, we have a large menu from which to choose.

Pink Bamboo Cafe has a lounge/bar area where you can get your favorite cocktail, beer or wine. Get served at the bar or enjoy your favorite drink before, while or after dining.

Pink Bamboo can cater your party also.  Please contact us for more information about our catering services.  We make deliveries to Portsmouth, Newington, Rye, New Castle, Kittery, Eliot.  Please call us to find out about delivery service.

We accept Visa, MasterCard and American Express.  Gift cards are also available for purchase.

We look forward to you next visit to Pink Bamboo Hot Pot Cafe.
